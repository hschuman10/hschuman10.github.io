Introduction to Skins

CS2 Skins are most closely related to NFT's. In the sense that they can be bought and sold for real money. When buying an item you get that specific item a 1 of 1 of sorts. Other items of the same type may exist but you would own the only one with the specified Pattern Index, Float value, and Paint seed.


What is a Pattern Index?

The Pattern Index determines the shift of the skin's pattern. It is denoted by a numerical value ranging from 1 to 1000. This characteristic holds significant importance for certain skins.

What is the Float value?

The Float value is a special parameter that determines how worn-out, scratched, damaged or dirty your skin looks in CS2. It has a value ranging between 0.000 to 1.

What is a Paint seed?

The Paint seed value, determines the positioning of pattern. Specifically, the paint seed determines the X offset, Y offset, and rotation value for the pattern position.
